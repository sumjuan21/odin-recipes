ASPARAGUS

Description

Roasted asparagus seasoned with garlic, lemon, and Parmesan cheese. This recipe is super quick to prep and roasting removes any bitterness.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch asparagus spears
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 220c
  2. place asparagus into a mixing bowl, drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat
  3. sprinkle with parmesan cheeese,garlic,salt and pepper
  4. arrange asparagusin a singe layer on a baking dish
  5. bake in oven until just tender, around 12 to 15 minuets
  6. remove from oven and sprinkle with lemon juice before serving
