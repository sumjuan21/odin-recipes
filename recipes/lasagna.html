Lasagna

Description

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with a creamy ricotta mixture, savory meat sauce, and lots of mozzarella cheese.This is a great freezer meal, make-ahead recipe, and leftovers freeze and reheat super well.

Ingredients

Cheese Fiiling

  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Meal Sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 3/4 lb ground italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz marinara sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp hot sauce
  • 1 tsp worcestershire sauce

Lasagna base

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2.5 cups mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Prep

  1. Combine the cheese filling ingredients in a medium bowl and set aside

make the meat sauce

  1. heat olive oil over medium heat and add the diced onions. let them soften for 10 minuets
  2. add the ground beef and sausage, increase heat to medium-high. Using a spactula break up the meat so tht is very fine and not crumbled. cook for 8-10 minuets. Add garlic during the last minuet
  3. add the chicken broth and use a spatula to clean the bottom and sides of the pot
  4. add marinara sauce, tomato pase, hot sauce and worcestershire sauce.Bring to a boul then reduce to a simmer. cover partially and let the sauce simmer gently while you boil the pasta noodles.

cook the lasagna noodles

  1. preheat oven to 190c
  2. begin boiling a large pot of salted water for the lasagna noodles.
  3. once pot starts to boil add the lasagna noodlesand cook according to package instructions
  4. drain and rinse lasagna noodleswith cold water until noodles are completely cool
  5. lay the cooled lasagna noodles flat on wax or parchment paper whole you beging assembling the lasagna

assemble

  1. spread 1 cup of the meant sauceon te bottom of a 9x13 inch casserole dish
  2. add 4 lasagna noodlesoverlapping them if needed
  3. spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles
  4. add 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce
  5. top with another 4 lasagna noodles, 1/3 of ricotta cheese mixture, 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce
  6. finish with 4 more lasagna noodles, the rest of the ricotta mixture and the rest of the meat sauce
  7. top with 2 1/2 cups of mozzarella cheese
  8. cover with foil before adding to oven

baking

  1. bake for 35 minuets
  2. remove foil and keep baking for another 10 minuets
  3. remove and garnish with fresh parslet
  4. let sit for 15 minuets prior to seerving to allow layers to set
